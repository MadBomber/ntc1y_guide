Mark 1:15 is Jesus' thesis statement. Everything that follows in the Gospels is an unpacking of these four compressed claims: the time is fulfilled (God's ancient promises are being kept), the kingdom of God is at hand (God's reign is breaking into the present), repent (turn around completely), and believe in the gospel (trust this good news with your life).

Notice that Jesus does not say the kingdom is coming someday. He says it is "at hand" — present tense, arriving now. The healings, the exorcisms, the calling of disciples this week are all kingdom actions. The future has invaded the present, and nothing will be the same.
